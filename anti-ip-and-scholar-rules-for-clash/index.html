Clash for Windows 归属地更改 & Scholar规则

2023年1月4日
 | 瞎搞

Parse脚本，可适配所有订阅

使用说明

复制到Parsers里，更新订阅即可

注：对所有订阅生效，如需针对单独订阅，请将- reg: ^.*$改为- url: <订阅地址>

归属地更改

针对部分网站显示IP归属地的流量分流规则

parsers:
- reg: ^.*$     ## 匹配所有订阅
  yaml:
    prepend-proxy-groups:
    - name: 🚩IP归属地
      type: select
    commands:
      - proxy-groups.0.proxies=[]groupNames|^((?!IP).)*$
    prepend-rules:
      - RULE-SET,anti-ip,🚩IP归属地
      
    mix-rule-providers:
       anti-ip: {type: http, behavior: classical, url: "https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/ferristale/anti-ip-attribution/generated/rule-provider.yaml", path: ./Ruleset/anti-ip.yaml, interval: 86400}

Scholar规则

学术网站不走代理

parsers:
- reg: ^.*$     ## 匹配所有订阅
  yaml:
    prepend-rules:
      - RULE-SET,scholar,DIRECT
    mix-rule-providers:
        scholar: {type: http, behavior: classical, url: "https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/ferristale/clash_rules_for_scholar/rules/scholar.yaml", path: ./Ruleset/scholar.yaml, interval: 86400}
